# The Fitness Formula - Personal Health & Fitness Project

Welcome to **The Fitness Formula**, a personal health and fitness project created by Sidhi for the School Exhibition. In today’s fast-paced and technology-driven lifestyle, many people struggle to maintain a healthy and balanced routine. This project aims to provide insights, guidance, and motivation for individuals to lead a healthier lifestyle.

## Project Overview

**The Fitness Formula** is designed to:  
- Help students and individuals understand the importance of fitness in daily life.  
- Provide simple steps and formulas to track physical activity, diet, and lifestyle habits.  
- Encourage people to make small, sustainable changes that have a long-term impact on their health.  

The project focuses on:  
- **Balanced Diet:** Tips and tricks for maintaining proper nutrition.  
- **Physical Activity:** Easy exercises and fitness routines suitable for all age groups.  
- **Healthy Lifestyle:** Encouraging good habits like proper sleep, hydration, and stress management.  

## Features

- Interactive charts and calculations to visualize fitness progress.  
- Clear and simple interface for ease of use.  
- Educational content that can inspire students and families to prioritize health.  

## Technology Used

- **HTML/CSS/JavaScript:** For creating a responsive and interactive web experience.  
- **GitHub Pages:** To host the website and make it accessible to everyone online.  

## How to Use

1. Open the website [here](https://creationsofsidhi.github.io/fitbot/)
2. Navigate through different sections to explore fitness tips, charts, and lifestyle guidance.  
3. Use the interactive tools to track your own progress and learn practical health habits.  

## Goal

The goal of **The Fitness Formula** is not just to provide information, but to **inspire action**. By understanding the science of health and fitness, students and visitors can make informed choices that improve their well-being in a sustainable way.
